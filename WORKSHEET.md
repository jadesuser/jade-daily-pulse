# Jade Daily Pulse — Workshop Worksheet

By the end of this hour you'll walk out with an AI agent that delivers a daily 1-page memo on your brand to your computer every morning.

---

## The frame

Think of this as briefing a new executive assistant. You hand them who you are, who your customers are, who you watch, what matters. Every morning they bring you what you'd otherwise miss. **That EA is the agent.**

---

## The 4 building blocks of Claude Cowork

| Block | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Project** | A workspace inside Cowork with its own files, instructions, and chat history. You'll create one today called *Jade Daily Pulse*. |
| **Skill** | A reusable set of instructions (`SKILL.md`) that tells Claude how to do a job. We wrote *Jade Daily Pulse* for you. **You can also make your own:** after walking through any repeatable task with Claude, ask "turn this into a skill" — Claude will package the workflow into a Skill you can reuse and share. |
| **Scheduled task** | Tells Cowork to run a prompt on a cadence. The schedule is what turns a tool into an agent. *Catch: only fires while your laptop is awake and Claude is open.* |
| **Connector** | Pre-built integration with services like Gmail or Drive. Optional. We'll use Gmail today so the memo lands in your inbox. |

---

## Commands you'll use today

| To do this | How |
|---|---|
| Make a project from a folder | Sidebar → **+ New Project** → **Create from folder** |
| Run the agent now | Type `Run the Jade Daily Pulse.` in chat |
| Schedule it daily | Type `/schedule` in chat |
| See / edit your scheduled tasks | Sidebar → **Scheduled** |
| Connect Gmail | **Settings → Connectors → Gmail** |
| Turn a workflow into your own Skill | After doing the workflow with Claude, type `Turn this into a skill.` |
| Change what gets surfaced | Edit `brand-profile.md` in your project folder |

## What you'll find on your memo every morning

1. **The dominant narrative** — the single loudest thing being said about you, with 2 real quoted sources.
2. **Three tensions** — places where two true things about your brand pull against each other.
3. **Where the world disagrees with your messaging** — the gap between how you talk about yourself and what consumers actually say.
4. **One early signal** — the thing nobody on your team is tracking yet.
5. **Competitor pulse** — 3–5 lines on what your competitors did in the last 24 hours.
