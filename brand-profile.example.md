## brand
Jade (thejade.shop)

## positioning
On-demand fine jewelry, made to order in a single studio. Recycled gold, recycled silver, recycled platinum; lab-grown or disclosed-origin stones. Heirloom-thinking, anti-speculation, anti-deadstock. The bet: customers will wait longer for a piece — and own it longer — when nothing is built on speculation.

## ICP
Design-conscious women 28–45, urban, sustainability-aware, already buying from Mejuri or Catbird and quietly frustrated by quality and inventory-flooding. Comfortable joining a waitlist for the right piece. Indexes high on Substack and design-publication readership rather than mass social.

## competitors
Mejuri, Catbird, AUrate, Vrai, Brilliant Earth

## topics to flag
recycled metals supply chain, lab-grown vs natural stone discourse, made-to-order / drop business models, ethical sourcing controversies, jewelry as anti-fast-fashion narrative, competitor launches and price moves

## sources you trust
r/jewelry, r/femalefashionadvice, Vogue Business (jewelry desk), Business of Fashion, Sotheby's Magazine, @diamondsanddoubt, @lookcurated, Future of Jewelry (Substack)
